There are many ways to get started with the murals.

First and foremost, take a look at the data! Once you are ready try out a tool like Palladio. Palladio helps you to better understand how you might map and visualize the data for your own project. Carto allows for quick and easy mapping.

Getting started with Palladio tutorial from Miriam Posner.

Palladio tutorial from a workshop by the UCLA Library and the UCLA Institute for Digital Research and Education.

Contact Andy Rutkowski (arutkows at usc.edu) or Stacy Williams (stacyw at usc.edu) to talk more about ideas for using the data and/or request a consultation or workshop
