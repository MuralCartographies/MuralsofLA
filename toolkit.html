There are many ways to get started with the murals:

Just check out the map and look around. Find out more about the collections and where they are.

Download the data and try it out in a tool like Palladio. Palladio helps you to better understand how you might map and visualize the data for your own project.

Getting started with Palladio great tutorial from Miriam Posner. Another Palladio tutorialfrom a workshop at UCLA by the Library and the Institute for Digital Research and Education.

Contact Andy Rutkowski (arutkowski at library.ucla.edu) to talk more about ideas for using the data and/or request a consultation or workshop
