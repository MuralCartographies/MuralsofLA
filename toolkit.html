There are many ways to get started with the murals:

Check out the map and look around. Find out more about the collections and where they are.

Download the data and try it out in a tool like Palladio or Carto. Palladio helps you to better understand how you might map and visualize the data for your own project. Carto allows for quick and easy mapping.

Getting started with Palladio great tutorial from Miriam Posner.

Another Palladio tutorial from a workshop by the UCLA Library and the UCLA Institute for Digital Research and Education.

Contact Andy Rutkowski (arutkowski at library.ucla.edu) to talk more about ideas for using the data and/or request a consultation or workshop
